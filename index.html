Care standards

Raised with a steady routine and a soft landing.

A great family puppy starts before go-home day. Camp Doodle focuses on daily handling, early confidence building, clean spaces, and honest conversations with each family.

Early socialization

Gentle exposure to household life helps puppies grow into curious, adaptable companions.

Veterinary guidance

Families receive clear health records and next-step care notes for their own veterinarian.

Lifetime mindset

Matching is based on temperament, lifestyle, timing, and the long-term needs of the puppy.
