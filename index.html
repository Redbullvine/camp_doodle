🩺

Each puppy receives a full vet exam, health records, and next-step care notes for your own veterinarian.

💉

Helpful details

Common questions

How do we get on the waitlist?

Send an inquiry below with your timing, preferred breed, and a little about your home. We will follow up with current plans and availability.

Can we choose a specific puppy?

Selections work best when temperament and family fit guide the decision. We will help you find the right match — and the quiz above is a great starting point.

What should we ask before reserving?

Ask about parent dogs, health records, go-home timing, coat type expectations, and what support looks like after pickup. We will answer every question honestly.

Where are you located?

We are a home-based breeder. Location details and pickup logistics are shared directly with families after initial contact.

What is included at pickup?

Health records, vaccination documentation, a care guide, feeding notes, and a familiar-scented item to help your puppy settle in.

Do you ship puppies?

We prefer in-person pickup so families can meet their puppy before going home. Reach out to discuss your situation and we will explore what works best.
